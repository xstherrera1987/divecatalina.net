Scuba Diving Courses

Experience Scuba

This course will allow you to try Scuba without the commitment of a full Scuba Course. Students are given a short class, dive briefing, basic skills and outfitted with gear and experience a dive in groups of two with direct Instructor supervision.


Scuba Diver

Course includes snorkeling skills, SCUBA gear assembly, gear selection and maintanence, basic SCUBA skills, buoyancy control, emergency SCUBA skills, dive planning and logging, entry and exit procedures, and rescuing skills for unconscious divers.


Advanced Scuba Diver

A course designed to hone previous diving skills. Divers will perfect buoyancy skills and basic SCUBA skills, learn navigation techniques, search and recovery skills, advanced dive planning, night or limited visibility diving, non-penetration wreck diving and deep diving. Once complete students will be NAUI certified Adanced Divers and trained to a maximum depth of 130 feet.


Nitrox Diver

A course designed to train divers in the use of EAN (Enriched Air Nitrox) Gas containing increased content and partial pressures of Oxygen to extend bottom times and increase diving safety. Upon completion students will be certified NAUI Nitrox Divers and trained in the use of Nitrox up to EAN40.


Master Diver

This is an excellent course to prepare divers for a leadership Certification. The emphasis of this course is to increase the students understanding and enjoyment by pratical application during a period of 6 Check Out Dives. Material covered in this course will be an comprehensive expansion of all the material learned in previous Diving Courses.


Other more advanced courses are available upon request. These courses include Night Diver, Deep Diver, Rescue Diver, Master Diver, Training Assistant, Assistant Instructor and Divemaster.
